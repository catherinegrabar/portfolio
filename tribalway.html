Tribalway

Overview

In 2016, I was approached by a group of engineers and PMs who needed UX help with solving a social problem: A lot of the popular social apps don’t facilitate in-person social interaction. These social apps serve as a bulletin board for people to share about their lives. Tribalway wanted to do something different and encourage social interaction in person. The team had a goal of connecting people to a “tribe” — a close knit group centered around an activity, a common interest or life situation. he mission was to get people together in person and not on screen and about planning what’s next rather than consuming what already has happened.

Process

My role was to take the problem of social isolation and the team’s mission and start creating ideas and wireframes of what a solution could be.
